What to know about Spring Hill

Rippa Villa

Rippa Villa, built in 1855, was one of the premier farms in Middle Tennessee. On November 29, 1864, the home and the families who lived there found themselves in the middle of one of the most dramatic events of the Civil War.

Parks and Recreation

A town favorite, the Splash Pad at Fisher Park hosts outdoor water play, a fantastic playground and three soccer fields.

Ultium Cells

The state-of-the-art Spring Hill plant will use the most advanced and efficient battery cell manufacturing processes. The plant will be extremely flexible and able to adapt to ongoing advances in technology and materials.
